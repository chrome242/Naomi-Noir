as as Audrey Sternwell

Well known in the local theatre scene, Suzy has been in dozens of student films and stared in many productions with the Actor’s Workshop of Ithaca. She's currently a member of the group, Comedy Flops and recently starred in the play Museum. Bad Faith was her second feature film.

as Mark

Masa graduated from Rutgers in Music and Linguistics, has a PhD in Linguistics from Cornell, and completed the two-year Meisner program at the Actor's Workshop of Ithaca. A trained vocalist, Masa has been in countless classical performances, operas, and on the local stage with Theatre Incognita, The Readers' Theatre of Ithaca , and the Melodramatics. He has also appeared on-screen in films, television, and webseries, most recently in the thesis film To Fall and Be Free. Masa has just written and directed his first short. Filmography at IMDb.

as Gail

Kristin is recently starred in the mystery farce, Moose Murders by Arthur Bicknell.
