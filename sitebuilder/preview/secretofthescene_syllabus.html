Teaching technique: Half of the class will be spent teaching new material, the
    other half will be used for writing exercises and work-shopping homework
    assignments.

    Course goals: to provide students with detailed knowledge of scene structure and
    types. At the end of the course students will have a variety of diverse scenes for
    portfolio use.

    Homework: homework is optional, but essential if you wish to have a comprehensive
    portfolio by the end of the 8 week course. All assignments can be e-mailed in
    Final Draft or Pdf format by Sunday evening to contact@naominoir.com for feedback
    by Tuesday’s class.


    Week 1: Scenes of Introduction

    Theme, genre, general overview of scene type, life dream, Scenes of Introduction,
    general overview of course

    Homework: Write a Scene of Introduction for a character without using dialogue


    Week 2: Scenes of Power

    Plot vs. character as ways to approach writing a script, how to get audience to
    identify with character, Enneagram, Power Struggle scenes, Scenes of Negotiation

    Homework: Scene of Negotiation


    Week 3: Scene s of Conflict

    Central conflict, Scenes of Hidden Conflict, Scenes of Open Conflict, Scenes of
    Betrayal

    Homework: Scene of Hidden Conflict


    Week 4: Scenes of Decision

    Visual Story-telling, telling story through action/ behavior rather than dialogue,
    visual descriptions  (as if/ like…), Scenes of Decision

    Homework: Scene of Decision


    Week 5: Scenes of Lies

    Dialogue, finding your character’s voice, how to avoid on-the-nose dialogue, words
    vs. action (or how to let your character lie), Creative Lie scenes

    Homework: Creative Lie scene


    Week 6: Scenes of Preparation

    Dialogue, finding your character’s voice, how to avoid on-the-nose dialogue, words
    vs. action (or how to let your character lie), Idea/ plan scenes, Scenes of
    Preparation

    Homework: Idea/plan scene


    Week 7: Scenes of Combination

    Scenes of Arrival, Character development scenes, Red Haring scenes, Smoking Gun
    scenes

    Homework: Combine scene of choice with existing scene from previous weeks


    Week 8: Scenes of Resolution

    Loglines, the Pitch, the Second Draft/ tips for editing, techniques for overcoming
    writer’s block, getting your script produced, Scenes of Resolution
