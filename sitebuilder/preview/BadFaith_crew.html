    Isaiah is the creator of Let's Create a Game, an on-line community of

    in Art at the University of Montana.
    When Christine was a child she was asked every year to write an
    essay on what she wanted to be when she grew up. She alternated
    between wanting to be a scientist and an artist. One year she said that
    she wanted to be both and was told by the teacher to pick one. Her
    first career was as a research biologist in plant pathology. Presently
    she is developing her skills in photography and the visual arts. Who
    says that you can't do both?
