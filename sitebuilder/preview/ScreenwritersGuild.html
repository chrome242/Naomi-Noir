The Screenwriter's Guild of Ithaca is a free screenwriting group, meeting every other
Thursday at 6 pm in downtown Ithaca. Bring pages to have read aloud, ideas to pitch,
problems to brainstorm, or simply enjoy a cup of coffee at College Town Bagels (on
the corner of N. Aurora and Seneca) while providing constructive feedback to other
members of the group. Depending on the number that show up each person will get 15 to
20 minutes to discuss their work.

No need to RSVP or make a commitment, just come when you can, we'd love to meet you!
